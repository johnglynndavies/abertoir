Abertoir 2021 Ticket Information

There are two pass options for Abertoir 2021:

• Full Festival Pass (£60) - this pass gave you access to the physical event as well as online access
• Virtual Only (£30/£35) - buy a virtual only pass on Eventive.

The two parts of the festival are mostly unique.  With some minor exceptions, there will be different content for the different parts. 

Full details for each pass type are available below. You can find information about our Covid-19 safety measures here.

FULL FESTIVAL PASS - £60

The Full Festival Pass gives you access to both the physical and virtual parts of the festival (2-7 & 12-14 November).

This pass can be purchased via the Aberystwyth Arts Centre box office, by phone (01970 623232). You will be allocated a seat in the auditorium. If you’re attending as a group or want to sit with friends, you will need to make one booking for all of these. If you have any requirements of preferences you can discuss these with the box office staff when making the booking. You must sit in the seats allocated to you.

You will be issued with a unique code which will allow you access a digital pass via Eventive. This code will be given to you, with information on how to redeem it, when you attend the physical event.

VIRTUAL PASS - £30 or £35

The Virtual Pass gives you access to the virtual part of the festival only (12-14 November).

This pass can be purchased directly via Eventive. There are two options:

• Individual (£30) – you will have access to the virtual part of the festival and you will receive a one copy of the festival brochure through the post
• Couple/Group (£35) – you will have access to the virtual part of the festival and you will receive two copies of the festival brochure through the post. This option is designed to support us with that little bit extra if more than one of you will be watching from the same location.

Passes were previously available at a lower, early-bird price (until October 29).

INDIVIDUAL TICKETS

Individual tickets are available for both the physical and online part of the festival.

Individual film tickets at the physical part of the festival are £6.90 each; available from Aberystwyth Arts Centre.

Individual film tickets at the virtual part of the festival are £7 each; available from Eventive.

REFUNDS

Full Festival Pass: If your circumstances change due to Covid-19 and you’re no longer able to attend the physical event, if you still wish to use your digital pass, we will partially refund your pass to reflect this. If you do not intend to use the digital pass, we will issue you with a full refund on the condition that the digital pass has not started and minus Eventive’s administrations costs.

Virtual Pass: If your circumstances change due to Covid-19 and you’re no longer able to attend the virtual event, we will issue you with a full refund on the condition that the virtual pass has not started and minus Eventive’s administrations costs (5%).
